What Is Truth?

Beyond opinions and beliefs — the unchanging, absolute reality

सत्यमेव जयते
Satyameva Jayate — Truth Alone Triumphs

Truth is not what we believe, not what we hope, not what is convenient. Truth (Sat) is what IS — the unchanging reality that remains when all illusions dissolve. It is eternal, self-evident, and beyond words.

How 8 Traditions Describe Absolute Truth

VEDIC TRADITION
सत् (Sat - Eternal Being)

Truth is existence itself — that which never changes, never dies. "Sat" is the first syllable of Sat-Chit-Ananda (Being-Consciousness-Bliss). It is Brahman, the only reality.

ISLAMIC TRADITION
الحق (Al-Haqq - The Truth)

One of the 99 names of Allah. Al-Haqq is ultimate reality — the truth that validates all existence. "God is the Truth, and what they call upon besides Him is falsehood" (Quran 22:62).

BUDDHIST TRADITION
Dharma / Tathātā (Suchness)

Truth is "things as they are" (Tathātā) — reality beyond mental constructs. The Four Noble Truths reveal the nature of suffering and the path to liberation from illusion.

TAOIST TRADITION
道 (Tao - The Way)

"The Tao that can be told is not the eternal Tao" (Tao Te Ching). Truth transcends language. It is the unnameable reality underlying all phenomena.

CHRISTIAN TRADITION
Aletheia (Unveiling)

"I am the way, the truth, and the life" (John 14:6). Truth in Christianity is not mere factual accuracy but divine reality revealed. Christ as Logos (Word) embodies ultimate truth.

JEWISH TRADITION
אמת (Emet - Truth)

The seal of God is truth (Talmud). Emet consists of the first, middle, and last letters of the Hebrew alphabet — symbolizing that truth encompasses all existence from beginning to end.

SIKH TRADITION
ਸਚੁ (Sach - Eternal Truth)

"Truth is the highest virtue, but higher still is truthful living" (Guru Granth Sahib). Truth is not just knowing; it is being and doing in alignment with divine reality.

ZEN BUDDHISM
直指人心 (Direct Pointing)

"A special transmission outside the scriptures, not founded upon words and letters." Truth is realized directly, not through concepts. It is your original face before you were born.
